About Me

Welcome! I'm Sara Adams, a soloist with the New York City Ballet. My journey in ballet began at age five, and dance has been my passion and purpose ever since.

Trained at the School of American Ballet, I joined NYCB in 2008 and was promoted to soloist in 2017. I've had the honor of performing lead roles in works by Balanchine, Robbins, and contemporary choreographers.

When I'm not on stage, I'm dedicated to teaching the next generation of dancers and advocating for arts education in schools.
